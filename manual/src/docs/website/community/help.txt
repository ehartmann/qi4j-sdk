[[help,Get Help]]
= Get Help =

Join the Google Groups qi4j-dev forum to discuss and get help, or help others on Stackoverflow.

If you found a bug or want to request a new feature, use the <<issue-tracker>>.

== Questions & Answers ==

To post question to and get answers from the Qi4j community, go to
http://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/qi4j[StackOverflow].

== Discussion ==

To discuss with the Qi4j community, it is easiest to do so at the Google Groups
https://groups.google.com/forum/?fromgroups#!forum/qi4j-dev[qi4j-dev forum].
